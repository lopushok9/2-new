About What Bird

An AI-powered bird identification application that helps you discover and learn about birds through advanced image recognition technology.

What Bird is a modern web application designed for bird enthusiasts, researchers, and nature lovers who want to quickly and accurately identify bird species from photographs. Using state-of-the-art artificial intelligence and computer vision technology, our app can analyze uploaded images and provide detailed information about the birds it detects.
